这个STM32程序适用于RobomasterA型板。程序主要有两个功能，第一个功能是通过CAN总线（ID为0x233）接收来自TX2的电机和舵机控制指令，并通过PWM输出控制电机和舵机；第二个功能是接收PI0(A)接口的高低电平跳变频率并通过CAN总线（ID为0x433）发布给TX2。
对于第一个功能，send.Data[0] 和 send.Data[1] 代表 转角 数据，send.Data[2] 和 send.Data[3] 代表 转速 数据，TIM4 的通道 1 和 2（TIM_CHANNEL_1 和 TIM_CHANNEL_2）：接收并输出 pwm_value1，用于 控制转角（Steering Angle）。TIM4 的通道 3 和 4（TIM_CHANNEL_3 和 TIM_CHANNEL_4）：接收并输出 pwm_value2，用于 控制转速（Speed）。TIM4的通道1、2对应A板的PD12（H）、PD13(G)，通道3、4对应A 板的PD14(F)、PD15(E)。


This STM32 program is designed for the Robomaster A board. The program primarily has two functions. The first function is to receive motor and servo control commands from TX2 via the CAN bus (ID：0x233) and control the motors and servos through PWM outputs. The second function is to receive the frequency of high and low level transitions from the PI0(A) interface and publish it to TX2 via the CAN bus (ID：0x433).
For the first function, send.Data[0] and send.Data[1] represent steering angle data, while send.Data[2] and send.Data[3] represent speed data. TIM4 channels 1 and 2 (TIM_CHANNEL_1 and TIM_CHANNEL_2) receive and output pwm_value1, which is used to control the steering angle (Steering Angle). TIM4 channels 3 and 4 (TIM_CHANNEL_3 and TIM_CHANNEL_4) receive and output pwm_value2, which is used to control the speed (Speed). TIM4 channels 1 and 2 correspond to PD12 (H) and PD13 (G) on the A board, while channels 3 and 4 correspond to PD14 (F) and PD15 (E) on the A board.
